EURO

The European Championship, commonly referred to as the UEFA Euro or simply the Euro, is the primary association football competition contested by the senior men's national teams of the members of the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA). Here is a brief history of the tournament: Early Years and Establishment 1958: The idea for a European national football championship was proposed by Henri Delaunay, the secretary of the French Football Federation. Unfortunately, Delaunay passed away in 1955, but his vision was carried forward.
